Safety Resources & Tips

Essential knowledge to keep you prepared and aware.

General Safety

  • Stay aware of your surroundings; avoid distractions like phone use while walking alone.
  • Trust your instincts; if a situation feels unsafe, leave immediately.
  • Share your live location with trusted friends or family when traveling.

Emergency Prep

  • Keep your phone charged and carry a portable power bank.
  • Memorize key emergency contacts and local helpline numbers.
  • Have a "safety word" with friends for discreet distress signaling.

Self Defense

  • Carry a personal safety alarm or whistle.
  • Consider taking a self-defense class to learn basic protection techniques.
  • Use keys or other objects as makeshift defense tools if necessary.
